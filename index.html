The Dastardly Computer

An evil computer has taken over your game of Battleship! Show this up-jumped punch card that humanity won't be conquered so easily and sink all of its ships!

Using your wired mouse peripheral, left-click an empty space on the computer's board to attack. If you hit a ship the space wil turn red, and if you sink it the entire ship will turn black! All ships are between 2 and 5 spaces long and must be in a straight line. Sink the evil computer's ships before it can sink yours and make humanity proud!
